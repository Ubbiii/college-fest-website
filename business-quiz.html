About The Event:

A high-stakes battle of business acumen where only the sharpest minds will thrive! Wisdom of the King challenges participants to showcase expertise in corporate affairs, marketing, finance, strategy, and global business trends. As the rounds progress, expect the unexpected—corporate case studies, business history, and real-time market scenarios. Speed, accuracy, and strategic thinking will be your keys to victory, but be prepared—surprise elements can shake up the game. Do you have what it takes to rise above the competition and claim the title of a top business strategist?

Event Rules:

  • The team should consist of 2 members.
  • Details and regulations of each round will be disclosed at the commencement of the respective rounds.
  • Participants must possess theoretical knowledge of the business world as well as current affairs.
