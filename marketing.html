About The Event:

Marketing is a game of persuasion, strategy, and storytelling. In BRAND BANDITS, participants must brand, promote, and sell products in the most innovative ways possible. From pitching outrageous ideas to managing PR crises and marketing failures, every round will push them to think fast and act smart. But beware—things aren’t always as they seem! Some challenges may demand selling the absurd or defending a catastrophic campaign. Only the most creative bluffers, sharpest strategists, and master storytellers will emerge victorious. Do you have what it takes to steal the spotlight?

Event Rules:

  • The team should consist of 2 members.
  • The Marketing event is designed to challenge teams to showcase their ability to execute tasks efficiently and craft compelling sales strategies that thrive in real-world market conditions.
  • Participants must have knowledge of marketing terminologies and strategies.
  • Details and regulations of each round will be disclosed at the commencement of the respective rounds.
