About The Event:

Welcome to Master of Accounts, a competition designed to test your proficiency in core accounting concepts. Participants must demonstrate their knowledge of journals, ledgers, trial balances, financial statements, and analysis techniques to solve real-world accounting challenges. Accuracy, speed, and problem-solving skills will determine who reigns as the ultimate Balance Breaker

Event Rules:

  • Each team must consist of 2 participants.
  • Participants should be familiar with journal entries, ledgers, financial statements, and ratio analysis.
  • Only basic calculators are allowed; programmable calculators and mobile phones are prohibited.
